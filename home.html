Ridge Michael Howe, who goes by the artist name RickySix60, is an American rapper, singer, designer, and songwriter based out of Chillicothe, MO. As early as 9 years old, he was inspired by Lil Wayne, Eminem, and others to pursue the art of Music and Lyricism. Today, he is making waves in the Midwest Underground scene and has traveled alongside artists such as Grizz Lee and Yxng Ched.
